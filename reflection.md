## What I Learned
Working on this project taught me how important data cleaning is before analysis. I practiced handling missing values, removing duplicates, and spotting outliers. I also became more confident using Pandas for data manipulation and Seaborn for clear visualizations.

## What Was Difficult
Dealing with inconsistent data types slowed me down and required extra care. Outliers were another challenge — deciding whether to remove or keep them wasn’t always straightforward and needed judgment rather than a fixed rule.
